Our basic website pricing can vary depending on the features and complexity required, but generally a simple brochure-style website with a few pages can cost anywhere from $150 to $300. This typically includes domain registration, web hosting,SSL certificate, and a basic content management system. More advanced websites with e-commerce, custom functionality, or extensive design work can cost significantly more.

The time it takes to complete a website can vary greatly depending on the scope and complexity of the project. A simple brochure-style website with a few pages can typically be completed in 2-4 days. More complex websites with custom functionality, e-commerce, or extensive design work can take 1-3 weeks or longer to complete. The timeline also depends on factors like the availability of content, client feedback and approval processes, and the workload of the development team. We work closely with our clients to establish a realistic timeline and keep them informed throughout the development process.
